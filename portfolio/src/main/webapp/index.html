ABOUT ME

Hello! I am a third-year computer science undergraduate from KNIT, Sultanpur. I have been a fan of Computer Science since my school days. Over the course of my engineering degree, I have tried my hands on various things like Android development, Computer architecture research, but most of my time has been spent in enjoying competitive programming.
